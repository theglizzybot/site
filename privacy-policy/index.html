Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Glizzy Bot ("we", "us", "our") respects the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

2. Information We Collect

When using Glizzy Bot, we may collect the following information:

  • Discord user IDs and usernames
  • Server configuration data
  • Chat relay messages (for functionality)
  • Error logs and usage statistics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the bot functionality
  • Improve and optimize the service
  • Monitor and troubleshoot technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the service. You can request data deletion at any time through our support server.

6. Third-Party Services

Glizzy Bot integrates with Discord and other third-party services. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies as well.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. For requests, please join our Discord support server.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the last modified date below.
